Data Mining NFL Score Prediction Project

Another recent project I have completed was a data mining project where me and two teammates developed a model using R and Rattle to predict the score of NFL games based on historic data from NFL games. We wanted to be able to predict the score of an NFL game based off the historic data and the sports betting lines to get an educated prediction of whether a game would be "over" or "under" the set line of total points by sportsbooks. We were able to develop a model with an MAE or mean average error of 6. This project allowed us to predict total points in games with relatively high accuracy and also gave us a chance to put our data mining skills at work with a real world example.
